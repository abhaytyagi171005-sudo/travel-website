About mussoorie

a picturesque hill station in Uttarakhand, India. Perched in the Garhwal Himalayas at an elevation of about 2,005 meters (6,580 feet), it offers stunning views of the Doon Valley and snow-capped Himalayan peaks, complemented by a cool, pleasant climate.
